Aurawin social computing.

Social networks for purpose.

You create and manage networks to share content based on purpose.  Networks are great for hosting projects, events, working groups, tasks, collections and more.  You can have as many networks as you can manage.

Sharing made simple.

Aurawin social networks are about sharing content with co-workers, friends, family, and loved ones.  Our permission based model allows you to control content while maintaining a positive environment.

Social applications.

Applications like Spectrum Cabinet™ organize social content.  They can provide access to things like files, pictures, videos, music, and documents.

The network manager app helps you create, manage networks, and accept or reject memberships to your networks.

Once networks are created, content and storage is available by way of network enabled applications.
